# 📡 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis & Prediction

## 📌 The Challenge

A leading telecommunications company is currently facing a critical challenge in maintaining its customer base, with the Churn Rate reaching an alarming **27.0%**. The primary issue is that the management lacks a clear, data-driven understanding of why customers are leaving. Without a predictive system in place, the company remains in a "reactive" state rather than a "proactive" one, leading to continuous financial losses and declining revenue.

## 🎯 Analysis Objectives

Analyze Customer Data at the following levels:

- Demographic
- Geographic
- Payment & Account Info
- Services

Study Churner Profile & identify areas for implementing marketing campaigns

Identify a method to predict future churners

## 📊 Key Metrics

- Total Customers
- Total Churn
- Churn Rate
- New Joiners
